About The Hobbit Hole

The Author

Hello! My name is Alyssa Gates, and this site was created to recommend some of my favorite books. Here you can find books ranging in reading level and genre, which have inspired me and brought me joy. I hope they can bring some inspiration and joy to your life too! If this is too grand a task to accomplish, I hope this list can at least provide a spark of amusement in your Sunday afternoon or just a moment with a good book.
